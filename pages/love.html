I have been known to cynically announce “Love isn’t real” in situations ranging from sappy TV shows, to the news of an engagement, to seeing two people hold hands.

But does love truly not exist? Do I lack the mental stability or capacity to feel deep, caring emotion towards another human being? To answer this question, I looked back at one week text messages.
